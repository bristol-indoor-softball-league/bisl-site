Latest News: 11th Nov 2016

There are a few things I've been asked to clarify before the league starts on Sunday...
Firstly, it has been noticed that an 'inside the park' home run is not always scored correctly... (4 for the number of bases reached, plus 1 for getting each runner, including the batter, who comes home) as per Pete's rule clarification last season. Further to this, can we ask our scorers to only mark HR (and circle the '5' on the scoresheet) for back wall home runs to ensure it is easier to identify those which qualify towards the home run champ competition.

Secondly, bunting. Much discussion as ever on its inclusion, and I am happy that we take a vote at the end of this season if there is the desire. However, it has been mentioned that some of the objection is from a safety perspective, where if a player shifts his hands on the bat as if to bunt, thus drawing the infielders closer, it could then be more dangerous to the fielders if the batter took a full swing of the bat. This 'fake bunt' is something that is disallowed in other indoor leagues around the country, and I therefore believe we should follow suit.

I understand that this is difficult to police, but if all players (who should appreciate the safety concern), and umpires (who should be able to warn any player who does not comply), are aware, we can hopefully remove this as a concern for everyone.
