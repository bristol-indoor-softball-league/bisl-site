  • League and Playoff games have 1 inning of 8 minutes batting each.
  • Playoff games have 1 inning of 12 minutes batting each (can reduce later games to 10 minutes if we are behind schedule)
  • The first team named is the home team, and they field first. For all Finals, home team is awarded for higher points scored in semi-final.
  • The team in the final column must supply an umpire and score-keeper for that game.
  • League games can end in a tie, playoff games must have a result.
  • Ties in league position and in playoff games are judged by:
    • total points scored so far
    • total points conceded so far
    • Coin toss
